Is a noun that meaning somenthing that is **heavy to carry** - either literally or emotionally. It can also be used as a verb, meaning to give someone a burden (to make them feel responsible or weighed down).

1. Literal meaning (noun):
	Something heavy that you carry or move
	Examples: *"The donkey carried a heavy burden of supplies"* - A physical load.

2. Figurative meaning(noun)
	A problem, responsibility, or worry that feels difficult to deal with.
	Examples: *"Debt can be a big burden"* - A financial problem that weighs you down.

2. As a verb ("to burden")
	Means to give someone difficult responsibility or problem.
	Examples: *"I don't want to burden you with problems"* - I don't want to make you feel responsible.
