Is a noun with two main meanings - one literal and one figurative

1. Literal meaning:
	A small amount of something - as much as you can hold in one hand
	Examples: *"He took a handful of grapes"* - The amount he could hold in his hand

2. Figurative meaning:
	A person or thing that's difficult to manage or control
	Examples: *"The puppy is a real handful"* - The puppy is playful and hard to handle
